Waldorf Astoria — Condos suites

Hello we're GCS, we rent luxury condominium suites. We know how hard It is to find a feel like home, place to stay. So let us take care of that, we're really good at it, we promise!

Highest Class

The Waldorf Astoria apartment condos suites are a perfect blend of luxury, efficiency and privacy to provide discerning residents an unparalleled level of comfort, luxury and service in Panama City.

Ready to deliver

All condominium apartment suite rentals include; internet connection, cable tv, water, and furniture. There is also a maid service available. Leases are available from six months to three years. They're all ready to deliver!

Benefits

All condominium apartment suite residences enjoy the same hotel amenities as the hotel guests. Including use of the heated sparkling pool, spa and fitness center, gift shop and a notable selection of restaurants and bars.

Best Location

Located in the banking area and the trendy Calle Uruguay section. Less than 15 minutes from the International Airport and within walking distance of the park on Cinta Costera as well as central business district.
